Getting Started.

Get started by downloading the latest zip archive(~0.5MB). The archive includes the source and needed images.

You can always use Notepad, TextEdit, or Gedit to make changes to the code. But here are a few fan favorites that may make your life a bit easier. If syntax highlighting is your thing, make sure "BrightScript" is offered.

Compatibility

Designed and tested with two second generation Roku LT (2400, and 2450) models.
